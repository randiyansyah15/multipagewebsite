Top 3 Movie Favourites

  1. Spiderman

    2. This film tells the story of Spiderman's origin and early life as a superhero. After perseverance by a genetically modified spider, shy and nerdy Peter Parker gains extraordinary spider-like abilities and adopts the identity of a masked hero to fight crime and injustice in New York, while facing a vengeful enemy, the Green Goblin.

  2. Pertaruhan

    3. After leaving prison, Elzan struggled to earn money through street fighting and becoming a debt collector, in order to forgive his family's house which was confiscated by the bank. However, defending the family home feels useless, because Elzan has to be in direct conflict with Ical, his own younger brother. With the help of Ara and Rio, Elzan fights for the integrity of his family by destroying everything.

  3. Ancika:Dia Yang Bersamaku

    4. This film will tell the story of Ancika, who is Dilan's new girlfriend after breaking up with Milea in high school. The story begins in 1995, when Dilan, a third year student, met Ancika, a high school student. Even though they are of different ages and at different levels of education, fate brought them together. In the film, Ancika is depicted as a beautiful, popular and brave girl. That's what made Dilan immediately fall in love with this 17 year old girl. Dilan also tries to use various methods to win Ancika's heart.
